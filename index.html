The GS PCM (Prelims + Mains) programme starts at ₹1.45 Lakhs + GST. This includes all study materials, access to recorded lectures, 40 Prelims test series, and Mains answer writing sessions. EMI options are available on request.
Yes, all our programmes are available in both Classroom (City Center, Gwalior) and Online formats. Online students get the same live lectures, recorded backups, and mentorship as classroom students. Tablets and course materials can be couriered on request.
We offer morning batches at 7:30 AM and afternoon batches at 2:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. A weekend batch is also available for working professionals. All batches have full lecture recordings available within 24 hours.
We hold monthly Parent-Teacher meetings where our academic team discusses your child's test scores, attendance, and areas needing improvement. You also receive a monthly written progress report and can contact your child's assigned mentor anytime via WhatsApp.
While we don't run a hostel directly, our counselling team helps outstation families find safe, verified PG accommodations within 1-2 km of our City Center campus, Gwalior. We can connect you with trusted accommodation providers who have housed our students for years.
Yes, we offer flexible payment options. Fees can be paid in 2-3 instalments for most programmes. For financially deserving students with strong academic records, we also have a limited merit-based scholarship programme. Please contact our admissions team for details.
